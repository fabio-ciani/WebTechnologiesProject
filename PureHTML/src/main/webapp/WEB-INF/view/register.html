Register to the application.

Your username must begin and end with a letter or digit.
The application allows choosing to use the period character or the hyphen character to create your username.
If you choose one of the aforementioned characters, then you have to continue to use it, and cannot use the other character.

Your password can contain any letter, digit and special character from the following list:

  • period (.)
  • hyphen (-)
  • exclamation mark (!)
  • dollar sign ($)
  • percent sign (%)
  • ampersand (&)
  • question mark (?)
  • hashtag (#)
  • commercial at sign (@)


The name, surname and username fields cannot be empty and are bounded to a maximum of 32 characters.
The password is limited to a minimum of 4 characters.
